Real Madrid vs Bayern Munich

Real Madrid have made it to the Champions League final by making a comeback in the 2nd leg match against Bayern Munich winning 2-1 courtesy of Joselu´s double in the last minutes of the game to win on aggregate score. They did so even though they were dominated by Bayern Munich during the game. The first half was dominated by Madrid with Vinicius Jr. hitting the post prior to Rodrygo having his shot saved by Neuer’s legs from the rebound. In the meantime Kane missed narrowly when his shot was deflected by Lunin. When things were at par Neuer made more excellent saves until Davies scored his maiden goal in Champions league through a quick counter attack after being brought in as a replacement.
